Pretentious Game

Price: PHP 69.95

Released: May 12, 2014

Pretentious Game is a short and story heavy game. Developed by Bari Silvestre under the name ‘Keybol Games’, it was the first game since 2014 that has received multiple positive reviews. From Mike Bithell, the creator of "Thomas Was Alone", which Pretentious Game is seemingly inspired from, to Vsauce, a very popular YouTube channel from which the publicity of the game has increased from.

Pretentious Game is a platformer where you follow the adventures of a blue square progressing through different obstacles to get to his pink square sweetheart and help him bring his feelings to light.
